# Facebook Page Checklist

## Setup
- Create Frontier business page.
- Add clear profile image and simple cover.
- Add short description: operations software for small contractors.
- Add contact email.
- Add website once beta signup is ready.
- Add privacy policy and terms links before public launch.

## Starter Posts
- "Building Frontier for small contractors."
- "What paperwork eats the most time after a job?"
- "Receipt OCR demo."
- "Estimate to invoice to payment workflow."
- "Looking for beta contractors."

## Content Rules
- Use plain language.
- Show real workflows.
- Avoid corporate SaaS wording.
- Ask for feedback before asking for signups.
- Do not promise perfect AI or guaranteed outcomes.

## Beta Post
Frontier is opening a small beta for contractors who want simpler software for clients, jobs, estimates, invoices, payments, documents, employees, and AI-assisted paperwork.

I am looking for users who will give direct feedback and tell me where the product is confusing.

Message the page if you want to be considered for the first beta group.

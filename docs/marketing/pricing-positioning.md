# Pricing Positioning

Frontier should be priced for small and medium blue-collar businesses: predictable, clean, and fair.

## Proposed Public Tiers
- Free: limited CRM trial.
- Starter: $20/month.
- Standard: $40/month.
- Professional: $60/month.
- Business: $80/month.
- Contractor Plus / Enterprise: $100+/month.

## Free Tier
Free should let users understand the workflow without creating meaningful provider cost.

Recommended limits:
- Clients, jobs, basic calendar: available with small limits.
- No free cloud document storage.
- Tiny AI demo allowance only if monthly metering is active.
- OCR, speech, image, and premium routing disabled until upgraded unless a controlled demo flag is added.
- Clear upgrade prompts before costly actions.

## Paid Plan Scaling
Scale primarily by:
- users
- AI allowance
- document/storage allowance
- OCR pages
- speech minutes
- image analyses
- route optimizations
- support level

Avoid crippling core business workflows. A solo contractor should still understand the full Frontier product.

## Abuse and Cost Protection
- Costly providers must be capability gated.
- Monthly quotas should be persisted before broad free AI trials.
- Admin kill switches should exist for OCR, speech, image, AI, routing, and storage.
- Suspicious usage should be logged for platform admin review.
